The aim of creating the website is to make our church be accessible to anyone through the internet. Any person who wishes to know about our church: from leadership/management, projects, church groups etc can do it at the comfort of his/her seat.

You can read all about our church history, future and present projects. Our service line-ups, various church groups and their leadership,etc. The website contains:

  • The L.C.C officials and their rankings.
  • Church groups and their functions
  • Explanations on church districts
  • Future church projects

PCEA Sabasaba Church Facebook Page

  • View Page

This is the official Facebook page for our church, created on 2nd September 2022. The MAIN aim for this is to spread the Gospel of Jesus Christ through an online platform. This way we get to reach many people out there who are available on Facebook. New people visiting Sabasaba town need a good church to fellowship in .

Our church being a church town, I saw the need to advertise it for the sake of those new people who come to settle at the nearest Sabasaba town either for work or other things. The page displays:

  • Both English and Kikuyu sermons LIVE as they happen, every sunday.
  • Motivational bible quotes.
  • Praise and worship sessions.
